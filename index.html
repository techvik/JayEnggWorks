Jay Engineering Works

Jay Engineering Works (ISO TS 16949 certified) is a precision metal parts supplier to the automotive industry specialising in turned parts, milled parts with secondary operations like turning, drilling , milling , honing , grinding , electroplating, phosphating , heat treatment etc.

High volume parts, repetitive machining,proven quality systems and customer focus on delivery and quality are the major strengths.

Precision machining of bar stock, castings, forgings using state of the art CNC machinery.More than 50 CNC machines for contract manufacturing.

State of the art resources comprising of CNC turning centers, turn mill centers, swiss turning centers,vertical machining centers , honing machines and a number of conventional production machinery helps in complete manufacture of parts as per print.

What We Do

Precision metal cutting operations involving turning,milling,grinding,honing,heat treatment,coating as per print.Turned parts, machined parts, CNC turned parts, precision turned parts with secondary operations like milling and drilling, turned parts with heat treatment, sliding head turned parts.A variety fo raw materials-steel turned parts, aluminium turned parts, brass turned parts etc



Jay Engineering Works is a 30 year old organisation specialising in supply of machined metal parts to the automotive industry. Precision parts, repetitive machining, high turned volume parts from bar stock, forgings and castings are manufactured as per print. On time delivery, repeatable quality and customer focus has been the key strengths.Large number of CNC machines ensures high level of repeatability and quality.

We are located in Ambattur, Chennai which is the automotive hub in India and it boasts of many multinational companies having their facility here. Ford, Hyundai , Renault,Delphi, Ashok Leyland, Visteon, Borg Warner are present in this city with huge facilities. There are more than thousands of Tier1 and Tier2 automotive companies in this area making it a developed region to source many parts.

ISO 9001:2000 standard is followed and documentation is maintained as per individual customer requirements.


Our Business

Our company has been the first choice for our customers for precision turned parts and for parts which involve turning, milling and secondary operations like heat treatment and electroplating. We have ISO 9001:2000 accredited suppliers for raw material,forgings, castings and ancillary services.

Who We Are

Our organisation is run by experienced promotors who have vast experience in the field of automotive ancillary business. Well experienced staff in product development and quality are our key strengths.Mass production of precision turned parts are our strenghts.


Services

Jay engineering Works has a large base of CNC machines which comprise of turning centers and vertical/horizontal machining centers apart from conventional machinery. Turned parts and machined parts are manufactured as per print. Most products are made from bar stock . Operations like turning, drilling, milling, grinding, 4th axis milling, honing, heat treatment, electroplating, phosphating, coatings are undertaken. CNC turning, Swiss turning, CNC milling, CNC drilling and tapping are our focus areas.Precision honing of close tolerance bores are done by Sunnen auto stroking honing machines to cater to high volumes.
Precision bores and high precision machining

As suppliers of of precision machining parts to the fuel injection industry we are capable of supplying parts with bore tolerances of less than 3 microns. Tandem bores, slotted bores can also be finished with high degree of accuracy.

ID Hex sockets with precision machining and polygon turning

We undertake ID Hex broaching in precision turned parts. ID hexagon sockets/square sockets .


Products

Turned parts like sleeves, adapters, capnuts, nuts, injector screws, fuel injection parts, cam rings,piston, secondary piston, banjo bolts,precision steel rivets, machining of SG iron castings like engine mounting brackets, knuckle, differential, machining of ferrous and non ferrous forgings are undertaken. Parts made from single spindle auto lathes, sliding head auto lathes, swiss turning lathes etc are undertaken by us. Free cutting steel like EN1A, 9SMnPb28, medium carbon free cutting steel like EN8M, C45,S45C,EN8D are used extensively and many parts have heat treatment and other finishing operations.

Infrastructure

Jay Engineering Works has all the infrastructure to consistently supply quality products to customers. Large pool of machining resources CNC turning Centers, CNC vertical machining Centers, ,sliding head turning, capstan lathes, drilling, grinding machines,honing machines, conventional autos and lathes etc.Highly productive machinery like Citizen sliding head swiss turning CNC lathe with back spindle, CNC turn mill centers with 11 live tools to make complex parts, vertical machining centers from Chiron, Kiwa with pallet changers for high efficiency, Sunnen honing machines for close bore tolerances and auto stroking facility for mass production.
